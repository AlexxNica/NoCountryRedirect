DOMAINS TO NCRify:
		Choose the domains that you want this extension to NCRify.

		Example:
		If you've set "blogspot.com" to be NCRified, this extension will
		always redirect you from country specific blogspot domains to
		'.com' addresses. So, if you type 'test.blogspot.jp' you
		will be taken to 'test.blogspot.com' instead.
