DOMAINS TO NCRify:
		Choose the domain(s) that you want to NCRify.

		Example:
		If you've set "blogspot.com" to be NCRified, this extension will
		always redirect you from country specific blogspot domains to
        '.com' addresses. Hence, if you type 'test.blogspot.jp' you
		will be redirected to 'test.blogspot.com'.
